Join YPHC

YPHC is open to motivated students who want to contribute to public health through real projects in research, education/advocacy, and media.

What you gain

Leadership Experience

Work within a structured team and learn how to run real initiatives.

Portfolio Deliverables

Leave with tangible outputs: campaigns, writing, resources, and projects.

Global Network

Collaborate with students across regions and build mentorship connections.
