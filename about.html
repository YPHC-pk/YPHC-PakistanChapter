About YPHC

The Youth Public Health Collaborative (YPHC) is a student-led global initiative dedicated to expanding access to public health opportunities through research, education, advocacy, and media.

Why we started

Many students interested in STEM and healthcare face barriers such as expensive programs, limited seats, and extremely competitive acceptance rates. YPHC was created to make real public health experience accessible — without requiring money, connections, or geography.

Vision

A world where every motivated student can contribute meaningfully to improving community health.

Core Values

Accessibility

Low-barrier opportunities designed for students everywhere.

Collaboration

Teams built to learn together and deliver real outcomes.

Service

Projects grounded in improving community health and literacy.
