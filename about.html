Our Story

Empowering Youth. Expanding Opportunity. Advancing Public Health.

What is YPHC?

The Youth Public Health Collaborative (YPHC) was founded to address two critical challenges: the need for stronger public health engagement and the limited accessibility of meaningful STEM opportunities for students.

Many students face financial or institutional barriers that prevent them from gaining research and leadership experience. YPHC bridges these gaps by providing an inclusive, student-led platform where youth can collaborate internationally on real-world health initiatives.
